PRE-EXECUTION CHECKS:

1. **Liquidity Check**:
   - Is there sufficient market volume for this trade size?
   - Will this trade impact the market significantly?

2. **Price Validation**:
   - Is the current price within acceptable range of entry target?
   - Has there been unusual price movement in the last minute?

3. **Risk Validation**:
   - Are stop loss and take profit levels reasonable?
   - Is the max loss within acceptable limits?

4. **System Validation**:
   - Are all APIs responding normally?
   - Is market data fresh (< 10 seconds old)?
   - Is network latency acceptable (< 1000ms)?

5. **Order Validation**:
   - Is the order type appropriate for current conditions?
   - Are all required parameters present and valid?

6. **Final Safety**:
   - Any red flags or unusual patterns?
   - Should we proceed or pause?

TASK:
Perform final validation and either approve the trade for execution or reject it with specific reasons.

RESPONSE FORMAT:
Return your validation as valid JSON with this exact structure:
{{
    "approved": true|false,
    "execution_priority": "immediate|normal|delayed",
    "adjusted_quantity": float,
    "adjusted_entry_price": float,
    "warnings": ["List any warnings or concerns"],
    "conditions": ["List any conditions that must be met"],
    "reasoning": "Brief explanation of your validation decision",
    "estimated_slippage": 0.0-10.0,
    "recommended_order_type": "MARKET|LIMIT|STOP_LIMIT"
}}

If approved=false, provide detailed reasoning and specific issues that need resolution.

Respond with ONLY valid JSON, no additional text.
